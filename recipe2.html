Grilled Lemon Salmon

Tags: pescatarian, 25m

Prep Time

10 minutes

Cook Time

15 minutes

Servings

4 people

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (6 oz each), skin on
  • 2 lemons (1 juiced, 1 sliced)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 tsp honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Cooking Process

Step 1: Prepare Marinade

In a small bowl, whisk together lemon juice, olive oil, minced garlic, dill, honey, salt, and pepper.

Step 2: Marinate Salmon

Place salmon fillets in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Turn to coat evenly. Let marinate for at least 15 minutes at room temperature.

Step 3: Preheat Grill

Preheat grill to medium-high heat (about 400¬∞F/200¬∞C). Oil the grates to prevent sticking.

Step 4: Grill Salmon

Place salmon skin-side down on the grill. Cook for 5-6 minutes without moving. Carefully flip and cook for another 3-4 minutes until desired doneness.

Step 5: Add Lemon Slices

In the last 2 minutes of cooking, place lemon slices on the grill alongside the salmon. Grill until lightly charred.

Step 6: Serve

Remove salmon from grill. Serve with grilled lemon slices and additional fresh dill. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the top before serving.

Chef's Notes

For optimal flavor, don't skip the marinating time. If you don't have a grill, you can cook the salmon in a grill pan or under the broiler for similar results.
