Creamy Tomato Pasta

Tags: vegetarian, 30m

Prep Time

10 minutes

Cook Time

20 minutes

Servings

4

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • tomato
  • cream
  • basil
  • garlic
  • onion
  • olive oil

Instructions

Step 1: Cook Pasta

Cook pasta according to package directions. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.

Step 2: Saut√© Aromatics

In a large skillet, heat olive oil. Saut√© finely chopped onion and garlic until fragrant.

Step 3: Simmer Sauce

Add tomato sauce and heavy cream to the skillet. Bring to a gentle simmer and stir in fresh basil.

Step 4: Combine

Add the cooked pasta to the sauce. Toss to coat. If the sauce is too thick, add a little of the reserved pasta water to reach your desired consistency.

Step 5: Serve

Serve immediately, garnished with extra fresh basil and Parmesan cheese if desired.

Chef's Notes

For a richer flavor, you can roast the tomatoes before making the sauce. The reserved pasta water is key to getting a creamy sauce that clings to the pasta.
