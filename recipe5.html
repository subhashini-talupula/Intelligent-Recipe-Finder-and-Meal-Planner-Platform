Vegetable Stir Fry

Tags: vegan, 15m

Prep Time

10 minutes

Cook Time

5 minutes

Servings

2 people

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce (or tamari for gluten-free)
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water
  • Sesame seeds for garnish
  • Green onions, sliced for garnish

Cooking Process

Step 1: Prepare Vegetables

Wash and chop all vegetables into uniform sizes to ensure even cooking. Mince garlic and grate ginger.

Step 2: Heat Wok or Pan

Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat until shimmering.

Step 3: Stir Fry Aromatics

Add garlic and ginger to the hot oil. Stir constantly for 30 seconds until fragrant but not browned.

Step 4: Cook Vegetables

Add harder vegetables first (carrots and broccoli). Stir fry for 2 minutes. Then add remaining vegetables (bell pepper and snap peas). Continue to stir fry for another 2-3 minutes until vegetables are crisp-tender.

Step 5: Add Sauce

Pour in soy sauce and rice vinegar. Toss to coat all vegetables evenly. Create a well in the center of the wok and add the cornstarch mixture. Stir until it thickens, then combine with the vegetables.

Step 6: Finish and Serve

Remove from heat and drizzle with sesame oil. Garnish with sesame seeds and sliced green onions. Serve immediately over rice or noodles if desired.

Chef's Notes

The key to a good stir fry is high heat and constant movement. Have all your ingredients prepped and within reach before you start cooking. Feel free to substitute with any vegetables you have on hand.
