Avocado Toast Deluxe

Tags: vegan, 10m

Prep Time

10 minutes

Cook Time

0 minutes

Servings

2 people

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 small lime, juiced
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp pumpkin seeds
  • Microgreens or sprouts for garnish
  • Extra virgin olive oil for drizzling

Cooking Process

Step 1: Toast Bread

Toast the bread slices until golden and crisp. You can use a toaster, toaster oven, or grill pan for extra texture.

Step 2: Prepare Avocado

Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl. Add lime juice, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mash with a fork until creamy but still slightly chunky.

Step 3: Assemble Toast

Spread the mashed avocado evenly over the toasted bread slices.

Step 4: Add Toppings

Sprinkle with red pepper flakes and pumpkin seeds. Top with microgreens or sprouts for freshness and color.

Step 5: Final Touches

Drizzle with a little extra virgin olive oil and add an extra pinch of salt if desired. Serve immediately.

Chef's Notes

For extra flavor, you can rub the toasted bread with a garlic clove before adding the avocado. Feel free to add other toppings like sliced radishes, cherry tomatoes, or a sprinkle of everything bagel seasoning.
