Chicken Caesar Salad

Tags: gluten-free, 20m

Prep Time

15 minutes

Cook Time

5 minutes

Servings

2 people

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, cooked and sliced
  • 1 large head of romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons (gluten-free if needed)
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Anchovy fillets (optional)

Cooking Process

Step 1: Prepare Chicken

If not already cooked, season chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Grill or pan-sear for 6-7 minutes per side until cooked through. Let rest for 5 minutes before slicing.

Step 2: Prepare Lettuce

Wash and thoroughly dry the romaine lettuce. Chop into bite-sized pieces and place in a large salad bowl.

Step 3: Add Dressing

Add about half of the Caesar dressing to the lettuce and toss gently to coat. Add more dressing as needed, but be careful not to overdress the salad.

Step 4: Add Toppings

Add most of the Parmesan cheese and croutons to the salad, reserving some for garnish. Toss gently to combine.

Step 5: Plate and Garnish

Divide the salad between two plates. Top with sliced chicken, remaining Parmesan cheese, and croutons. Garnish with lemon wedges and freshly ground black pepper.

Step 6: Serve

Serve immediately with extra dressing on the side for those who want more.

Chef's Notes

For the best results, make sure your lettuce is completely dry before adding dressing. This prevents the dressing from becoming watery. If you have time, making your own Caesar dressing from scratch will take this salad to the next level.
